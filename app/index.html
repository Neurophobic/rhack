Calling data wizards, hackers, GIS Geeks, machine learning chicks and AI lovers.

You’re invited to help investigate to the application of AI to remote sensing, satellite imagery, mapping, drone and crowdsourcing for disaster resilience.

This Hack is a 24 hour sprint to explore how the latest tools in data science and Earth observation can save lives and reduce the negative impact of natural disasters and climate uncertainty.

We’re not going to come up for air until we’ve hacked our way through fires, floods, hailstorms, droughts and cyclones.

If you’ve always wanted to be a superhero, this is your chance (just get an algorithm to do the heavy-lifting bit.)

Why are we doing this?

How can we use AI and Earth Observation data to better mitigate the effects of natural disasters?

What if local communities could fully understand and interact with value added satellite imagery and remote sensing, in preparation, during and after a critical situation?

It’s even got a name: Geospatial intelligence.

Who’s invited?

We’re looking for individuals who have a passion for: data, statistics, problem solving, machine learning and management initiatives when faced with natural disasters:

  • software developers
  • UX designers
  • technologists
  • data scientists
  • GIS experts

What’s the Agenda?

The GEOSPATIAL INTELLIGENCE HACK will begin with a hit of caffeine at 6.00 pm on Friday the 6th of January, followed by food at 7pm, accompanied by a briefing kit and goodies to prepare you for the upcoming hours…

If you have arrived with a project already in mind, you will pitch it to the rest of the hackers and will have the chance to add other members to your team (if necessary!). If you have not arrived with a project already drafted, Friday night is the night to come up with your solution and group with others.

Saturday consists of working with your team and building on your project (after a lovely breakfast), to make it the best it can be, to then present it at around 8pm on Saturday night (followed by drinks and food). See below for a little more detail.

Where’s it being held?

The GEOSPATIAL INTELLIGENCE HACK will be held at the British Interplanetary Society in Vauxhall, London

Address: 27 – 29 S. Lambeth Road, London, SW8 1SZ

What do I need to bring?

We’ll provide the hacking environment and ‘briefing kit’ you require, such as hardware; Nvidia Dev box and Jetson TX1, helpful APIs/databases, and a run through of each disaster and the respective major players – so you get the whole picture…

…All YOU need to do is bring your own computer and any other hacking tools you deem necessary!
